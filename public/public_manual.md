# Camping Gear Inventory & Loan System 🏕️

Welcome to CampSync! This tool helps you log your personal camping equipment, share it with friends, and borrow gear for your next outdoor adventure. 

---

## 🎒 Getting Started

### Managing Your Gear
To lend items out, you first need to catalog them! 
1. Navigate to the **Inventory** dashboard.
2. Click **+ Add New Gear**.
3. Fill out the **Name**, **Description**, and **Initial Condition** of the item.
4. Set the **Visibility**:
   - **Public**: Anyone on the platform can see and request to loan this item.
   - **Private**: The item is visible only to you (useful for personal cataloging!).
5. Add custom **Tags** (e.g., `tent`, `winter`, `cooking`) to categorize your equipment.
6. The item will now appear under the **My Gear** tab.

### Loaning Gear
Ready for a trip? You can browse gear listed by other users!
1. Go to the **Inventory** dashboard and select the **Global Rental List** tab.
2. Browse or search for items currently marked as **Available**.
3. *Future Implementation:* In a coming update, you will be able to click directly on an item to request a loan. 

---

## 🌟 The Karma System

CampSync rewards users who actively participate in the community! When you loan a piece of your equipment to another registered user through the platform, you generate **Karma Points**.
- **Loaning**: Earn points when a borrower safely returns your item. 
- **Leaderboard**: See who the top community contributors are by checking the **Karma Leaderboard** in the sidebar. 
- *Note:* Karma points are not awarded for external rentals (loaning to someone not on this app).

---

### Troubleshooting

- **Item Not Listed:** If you don't see an item you expect, the owner may have set it to `Private` or it might have been unlisted by an Administrator.
- **Can't See Admin Dashboard:** The `Admin Dashboard` button only appears for users assigned the Administrator role.
- **Reporting Issues:** Contact your system administrator if you encounter bugs or malicious behavior.

## 🤝 Dispute Arbitration

We track the condition of gear before and after a loan. 
1. When requesting an item, the loanee will log an **Observation** of the gear's state upon receipt.
2. When returning an item, the owner can log a **Status Update** describing the return state. 
3. If the owner and loanee disagree on whether damage occurred, either party can request **Admin Arbitration**. A site administrator will review the logs.
